Our Menu

Chicken

Our free-range chicken is sourced from local farms and prepared with a blend of aromatic herbs and spices. Whether grilled, roasted, or sautéed, every dish delivers rich flavor and juicy tenderness.

Signature dishes include: Herb-Roasted Chicken, Honey-Glazed Chicken Thighs, Lemon-Pepper Grilled Breast, Nashville Hot Tenders, and Chicken Piccata with capers and white wine.

All chicken entrées are served with your choice of two seasonal sides and house-made dipping sauces. Ask your server about our rotating weekly special.

Beef

Prime-grade beef, aged to perfection. Our cuts are hand-selected by our executive chef every morning from trusted regional ranches that prioritize sustainable, grass-fed practices.

Signature dishes include: 12oz Ribeye with chimichurri, Slow-Braised Short Rib, Korean BBQ Bulgogi Bowl, Classic Beef Bourguignon, and our famous Double Smash Burger.

Every beef dish is cooked to your exact temperature preference. We recommend medium-rare for maximum tenderness, but our kitchen accommodates all preferences without compromise.

Sushi

Our sushi program is led by a trained itamae with over 15 years of experience. We receive fresh fish deliveries daily, ensuring uncompromising freshness in every bite.

Signature rolls include: Dragon Roll, Spicy Tuna Crunch, Rainbow Roll, Salmon Avocado Maki, and our house-special Volcano Roll topped with baked scallop and sriracha aioli.

Omakase tasting menus are available Tuesday through Sunday evenings with 48-hour advance notice. These chef-curated experiences pair seasonal selections with premium sake flights.
